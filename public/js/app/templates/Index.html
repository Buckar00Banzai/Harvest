AFRIKA TEMBO

A Visual Journey for the Long Life of African Elephants

AFRIKA TEMBO is a documentary project by visual anthropologist Carolina Caff√© and documentary photographer Fernanda Lenz. The team will spend a month in Tanzania producing both a web series and a visual journal about the rapid decline of the African elephant population, and their risk of extinction within the next few decades.

Does Africa have to choose between modernization and wildlife? The project will document how organizations and communities are joining efforts to promote development hand in hand with conservation.

Through the power of filmmaking, we seek to awaken a sense of responsibility for our planet and inspire people to take action on local and global issues that are ultimately connected and affect each one of us.
