AFRIKA TEMBO

A Visual Journey for the Long Life of African Elephants

AFRIKA TEMBO is a documentary project by visual anthropologist Carolina Caff√© and documentary photographer Fernanda Lenz. During a month in Tanzania the team will produce a web series and a visual journal about the rapidly decline of african elephant with the risk to be extint in the next few decades.

Does Africa have to choose between modernization and wildlife? The project will document how organizations, authorities and communities are joining efforts to find economic solutions that can ensure that conservation goes hand in hand with productivity.

Through the power of filmmaking, we seek to awaken a sense of responsibility for our planet and inspire people to take action for local and global issues that ultimately are connected and affect each one of us.

The documentary production has been possible with a initial support but is still in need of funding. We ask for your help and we thank you with special gifts!
